Who we are

A learning and innovation platform dedicated to equipping the next generation.

YRL Innovation Lab is a learning and innovation platform dedicated to equipping individuals with the mindset, skills, mentorship, and practical experience needed to solve real-world problems and create lasting value. We believe the future belongs to people who can learn continuously, adapt quickly, and use technology responsibly to improve their work, businesses, ministries, and communities.

Our programs combine practical learning, project-based experiences, mentorship, and Artificial Intelligence to help learners grow into capable innovators. We don't focus on delivering information — we focus on developing capable people who keep growing long after their program has ended.

Why we are different

Most platforms deliver information. We develop capable people.

Our goal is not only to teach you new skills — it is to help you become a lifelong learner who keeps growing as technology evolves.

01 · Daily Check-ins

We check on you every day.

Every student completes a short daily check-in. Nobody disappears unnoticed. Nobody gets stuck alone in silence.

02 · Peer Learning Groups

5–8 people on your exact same path.

You are placed in a small group learning the same skill at the same time. You help each other and celebrate progress together.

03 · Personal Mentorship

A mentor who follows up when you go quiet.

Matthew and our mentors monitor every group. If someone goes quiet, we follow up personally. Nobody gets left behind.

04 · Public Accountability

Document your journey. Share it publicly.

Students document their learning and share it on social media. When your progress is public, quitting has a cost.

05 · Live Webinars

Regular sessions to reinforce your learning.

Live webinars go deeper than self-paced materials, answer real questions, and keep the community's energy alive.

06 · Real Projects

You build things. Not just watch things.

Every learning track includes real projects — tangible outputs you can show, use, and build on. Learning that produces nothing changes nothing.

Meet the founder

Matthew Folawe O.

Creative Designer · AI Visual Specialist · Technology Educator · Community Builder

"I believe the future belongs to people who can identify problems, embrace innovation, and build meaningful solutions. My vision for YRL Innovation Lab is to raise a generation that doesn't just use technology — but creates it to transform lives, strengthen communities, and inspire positive change."

Matthew started YRL Innovation Lab out of a simple conviction: that technology, in the hands of young people who love God, becomes a tool for real change — not just personal gain. After years working at the intersection of design, AI, and education, he set out to build a community where skill and faith grow side by side, and where every project carries a purpose bigger than the portfolio.

Today, YRL exists to turn that conviction into a curriculum — one cohort, one builder, one bootcamp at a time.
